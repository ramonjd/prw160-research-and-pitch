This site has been tested in all modern browsers and is mobile ready.

Why this website?

While conducting my research for the Industry Analysis component of this module, I was struck by just how important online publishing and promotion is to the success of a new author. The marketing aspect aside, simply having your voice and work available to a potential audience of millions 24/7 is crucial for the development of future networks and your own brand. Building a website to feature my research was therefore the only logical presentation format.
